About Thinner Mart
India's trusted online destination for premium quality thinners at factory prices. We're here to make thinner buying simple, affordable, and transparent by eliminating middlemen. Whether you're a retailer, paint shop, or industrial buyer, Thinner Mart is designed to serve your needs directly from the source.
Backed by the legacy and expertise of Mehta Corporation, a well-established name in the thinner manufacturing industry since 2005. With nearly two decades of hands-on experience, Mehta Corporation brings trust, consistency, and uncompromising quality to this new digital platform.
Thinner Mart was born out of a simple yet powerful idea. To deliver quality thinners directly to businesses at the best prices. We believe every business, big or small, deserves access to high-quality products without paying a premium.
